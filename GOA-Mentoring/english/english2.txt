3. My Favorite Food
My favorite food is pizza. I like it with cheese and tomatoes. I eat pizza every Friday with my friends. It tastes delicious!

4. My Best Friend

5. My School

6. My Hobbies

8. My Pet

9. A Trip to the Zoo

10. My Dream Job
I want to be a doctor. I like helping people. Doctors work hard, but they make people feel better. I will study a lot to be a good doctor.
